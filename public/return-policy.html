Return & Exchange Policy

Our Promise

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not satisfied, you may return or exchange most items within 7 days of delivery.

Eligibility

  • Item must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition.
  • Tags and original packaging must be intact.
  • Sale items and final clearance products are not eligible for return.

Return Process

  1. Contact our support team via support@yourstore.com within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Include your order number and the reason for return.
  3. We will respond with return instructions and the return address.
  4. You are responsible for return shipping unless the item was damaged or incorrect.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange for a different size or color, please contact us to confirm availability. We'll reserve the item while we await your return.

Refunds

  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method.

Damaged / Incorrect Orders

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We’ll make it right at no cost to you.
